
1- In which ways do the OO model and the Relational model conflict?
	 
	 - Inheritance (OO has it, Relational not)
	 - Relationships / associations (OO has 7 types relational has foreign key) 
	 - Identity (OO has built in / relational has primary key)
	 - Hot to access (OO has associations / relational has foreign key)
